Frequently Asked Questions

1. What products do you sell?
We sell laptops, smartphones, smartwatches, wireless earbuds, keyboards, monitors, and computer accessories.

2. How can I place an order?
Customers can place orders through our website by selecting products, adding them to the cart, and completing secure payment.

3. Which payment methods are accepted?
We accept Credit Cards, Debit Cards, UPI, Net Banking, and Wallet payments.

4. Can I cancel my order?
Yes. Orders can be cancelled before they are shipped.

5. How can I track my order?
You can track your order from the "My Orders" section using your Order ID.

6. Do you provide international shipping?
Currently, we provide shipping only within India.

7. What should I do if my payment fails?
Wait for 30 minutes. If the amount is deducted and the order is not confirmed, contact customer support.
