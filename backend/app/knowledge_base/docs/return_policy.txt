Return and Refund Policy

Customers can request a return within 7 days from the delivery date.

Products must be:
• Unused
• In original packaging
• Include all accessories
• Include the original invoice

Refund Timeline:
• Credit Card: 5-7 business days
• Debit Card: 5-7 business days
• UPI: 2-3 business days
• Wallet: 24 hours

Non-returnable Items:
• Gift Cards
• Downloadable Software
• Damaged products due to misuse

Exchange Policy:
Products with manufacturing defects can be exchanged within 10 days.
