Troubleshooting Guide

Laptop does not start
1. Check charger connection.
2. Verify power outlet.
3. Hold power button for 15 seconds.
4. Charge for 30 minutes.
5. Contact support if issue persists.

Laptop overheating
1. Close unused applications.
2. Clean air vents.
3. Place laptop on a flat surface.

WiFi not connecting
1. Restart router.
2. Restart laptop.
3. Forget and reconnect network.
4. Update network drivers.

Bluetooth not working
1. Enable Bluetooth.
2. Restart Bluetooth service.
3. Update Bluetooth drivers.

Battery draining fast
1. Reduce screen brightness.
2. Close background applications.
3. Enable battery saver mode.
